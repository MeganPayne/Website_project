Piemajas Zoo BREZI FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

  • 1) Can I bring my own food to the Zoo to feed animals?

    • No, each animal is being fed with food specifically designed for the animal and their daily intake is being monitored to reduce the risk of obesity. However, you can buy fresh carrots and grain granules in the Zoo to feed the animals.



  • 2) Do I have to reserve the BBQ places in advance?

    • Yes, all reservations are confirmed via phone +371 22005519. We advise to call at least one month in advance to ensure the BBQ place reservation.



  • 3) How much does it cost to reserve a BBQ place?

    • BBQ place reservation is free. However, if you make the reservation and there is change of plans, please, cancel your reservation immediately via +371 22005519.



  • 4) Can I bring my own music?

    • No, we do not allow loud music in the Zoo as it can cause stress to the animals and disturb other visitors.
