Welcome to Zoo Brieži!

Mini zoo "Brieži" was established in 2013 by its owner Signe Utāne. Zoo is opened all year through, however, due to weather conditions, not all animals can be viewed during winter, late autumn and early spring. In the zoo, visitors can view a wide range of different animals that have originated from all over the world. There are Guinea pigs, rabbits, squirrels, several different breeds of hens, pheasants, geese, peacocks, sheep, goats, ponies, deers, a racoon and a donkey. Children can go for rides with ponies and the donkey. Zoo also offers BBQ places with or without a roof that can be reserved in advance. It is possible to rent out a grill as well. For smallest visitors, there is a children playground in the centre of the Zoo territory.
